About Me

I am a UI/UX designer and computer technician. I also enjoy many hobbies like guitar, model trains, gold prospecting to name a few. I've been in the printing industry since the late 80's and jumped on the computers before most people even knew how to turn one on. As you can imagine I have extensive knowledge of design and print. When the job no longer challenged me I did much research on a new craft that would be challenging and fun at the same time and UI design seemed to fit the bill perfectly. I currently reside in Santa Paula, California about fifty miles north of Los Angeles. Santa Paula is a small town but I can be in the heart of LA in a little over an hour. I like the weather and the small-town feel with anything important like the beach or a concert just a small drive away.
